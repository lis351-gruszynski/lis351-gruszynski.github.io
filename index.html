Welcome, I'm Chloe!

I'm pursuing a double major in Marketing and MHR (management and entrepreneurship) and obtaining a certificate in Digital Studies. In the Fall of 2021 I completed a Digital Marketing Professional Certificate from the University of Wisconsin. Through the ten month course I've had the ability to learn a handful of skills, some notably being web development, search engine optimization, email marketing and social media marketing.

My future plans include starting my own digital marketing agency working for small businesses on building their digital presence and improving advertising strategy.
