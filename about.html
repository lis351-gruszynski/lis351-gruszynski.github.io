A little more about me...

My involvement at UW-Madison

Women in Business Techonology

WIB-Tech strives to increase female representation and break down barriers in the business technology industry while also painting a better picture of what the future environment will look like for women entering the field.

American Marketing Association - UW-Madison Collegiate Chapter

AMA is a professional association for students interested in leading the practice, teaching and development of marketing knowledge worldwide. Our principle role is to serve as a forum to connect like-minded individuals and support marketing practice.

Ad Club

Ad Club is a group of students looking to challenge ourselves, make connections and share our passion for advertising. Our goal is to build the next generation of advertising professionals by preparing members with unique and formative experiences, similar to that of an agency.

Hypnotiq: Hip Hop Dance Crew

Hypnotiq is a club dance team that performs at many events on and off campus. With a focus on appreciating diversity and giving back to the community, Hypnotiq provides dancers with an opportunity to develop, choreograph, and perform.
